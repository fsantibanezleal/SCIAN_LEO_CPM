CPM Simulator Guide

Getting Started

  1. Click Initialize to create the cell population
  2. Click Start to begin the simulation
  3. Use Step for frame-by-frame advancement
  4. Adjust parameters in the controls panel

Cell Parameters

Filopodia: Number of Gaussian protrusions per cell. More filopodia = more irregular cell shape.

Velocity Scale: Controls how fast cells move. Higher values = faster but less stable simulation.

Contour Points: Resolution of the cell membrane. Higher = smoother but slower.

Environment

EVL: The Enveloping Layer boundary migrates downward, pushing cells.

DEB: The Dorsal Epiblast boundary converges upward.

Mechanotaxis: When enabled, cells experience a directional bias force toward stiffer substrate.

Visualization

Cells are colored by their index. Toggle contours, centers, and boundaries independently.
